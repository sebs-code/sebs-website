Projects

Sebastian is a software engineer and technologist who has spent over fifteen years building software across healthcare, media, and finance. In 2013 Sebastian founded Datablast - a software consultancy, with clients as diverse as the NHS, Global Media, Strava, Barclay's and Schroders.

Outside of client work, he enjoys bringing novel approaches (such as behavioural science) to software design in order to create unique products - a process he calls "tinkering with convexity".

He holds an MA from the University of Chester, an MSc in Software Engineering from the University of Oxford, and is a Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts. He enjoys hobby photography, and lives in London.
