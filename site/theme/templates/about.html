About Sebastian David Lees

Sebastian is a software engineer and technologist who has spent over fifteen years building software across healthcare, media, and finance. In 2013 Sebastian founded Datablast - a digital product consultancy, with clients as diverse as the NHS, Global Media, Strava, Barclay's and Schroders. He enjoys bringing novel approaches (such as behavioural science) to software design in order to create unique products.

He holds an MSc in Software Engineering from the University of Oxford, and is a Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts. He enjoys hobby photography, and lives in the Cotswolds.
